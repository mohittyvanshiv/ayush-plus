Questions

Frequently asked

How quickly can treatment begin?

Once we receive your medical reports, you'll have specialist opinions and a treatment plan within 48 hours. Most patients travel within one to three weeks, depending on visa processing and medical urgency.

How do costs compare with private care at home?

Every patient receives a transparent, itemised estimate before travelling — covering treatment, hospital stay, and our concierge services. Costs at India's premium private hospitals are typically a fraction of equivalent private care in the US, UK, or Gulf, with no compromise on technology or expertise.

What about language and communication?

Dedicated interpreters are available for Arabic, French, Russian, Swahili, Bangla, and other languages, and your care manager communicates with your family in your preferred language throughout.

Can my family travel with me?

Yes — we arrange visas, accommodation, and local support for accompanying family members, including extended stays during recovery.

What happens after I return home?

Your care manager schedules tele-consultations with your treating specialist, coordinates with your local physician, and remains your point of contact for any concern during recovery.

Is my medical information kept confidential?

Absolutely. Records are shared only with the treating medical team, and all staff and interpreters operate under strict confidentiality agreements.
